Co owning Diya Pool

Document Status: in draft.

Why?

Currently, after the owner have started operating pool for 3 months. he sees that it's very hard and nearly impossible to invite people holding ADA to come delegate to the pool.
The reasons for that are

  1. In order to mine a block every epoch, the pool needs roughly at lease 1.5M ADA,
    since we are very small pool with the current stake around 9k,
    which that info new delegators or potential delegators will see that, there are no profit staking to this small pool.
  2. Given the first reason above, even if the pool margin fee is set to 0, current delegators are moving out their delegations to another pools.
    Since this poool could not mine the block regularly every epoch.
  3. Given a chance of 0.1% - 0.2% per each epoch to mine a block. The owner can not operate a pool without without loss.
    The current cost for running the pool is around USD 80 - USD 100 per month.
Given the above reasons, the pool owner has decided to invite people who own a good amount of ADA to share own the pool.
Once you own a share of the pool, you will get more rewards than other delegators who delegate to the pool,
where the amount of more rewards are given up per the share percentage of the pool.

Benefits

By owning the DIYA pool shares, you will get special rewards for each epoch the pool mined a block successfully.
The special rewards will be given out in a day or two after the epoch rewards have been given out.

How special rewards are calculated

In every epoch, after the stake pool rewards have been given out to the delegators (this is just normal stake pool reward).
Then, the owner, deducts the cost of running a pool for each epoch from the stake pool fee,
then the owner distributes the rest of the fee to the pool's shareholders, the shareholders will receive the same amount percentage of owning share of remaining fee in ADA.

example:
Given in the current epoch: The fix fee amount received is 340 ADA (current minimum fee).

  • total_fee = 340 ADA
  • fee_after_deduct_cost = 340 - 10 = 330 ADA
    * deduction of 10 ADA is approximation based 2USD = 1 ADA.
    * in one month there is roughly 6 epochs, so to cover cost of 100 USD per month. 10 ADA is deducted each epoch
  • Now if you own 10% share of DIYA pool
    your additional rewards will be = 10% of 330 = 33 ADA

How?

In order to allow people to own shares of the pool, there will be a total 1,500,000 (one million and five hundred thousand shares) shares in total.

Share Distributions

  • 5% (75,000 shares) are owner reserve shares
  • 95% (1,425,000 shares) are co-owners' shares

How can I own some shares

In order to own some amount of shares you must agree to deligate the same amount of ADA as a minimum amount of ADA to DIYA pool, during the term as per written below in the following section.

Terms

Co owning contract time: 1 year term, i.e., starts: July 1, 2021, ends: July 31, 2022
